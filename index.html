#PhoenixSummit2024

Phoenix Summit stands as a pivotal national Cyber Security event dedicated to advancing both defensive and offensive security realms and shaping the future of this critical field. This summit gathers an illustrious mix of national and international experts, including cyber security researchers, policymakers, corporate leaders, and innovative solution developers. It acts as a dynamic hub for knowledge exchange. Catering to a broad spectrum of freshers and professionals, our two-day program is segmented into Blue Team Day and Red Team Day with Closing Ceremony. With an anticipated audience of about 1000, including 25 distinguished speakers and guests from government, academic institutes, and corporations, Phoenix Summit is set to become a landmark in history. It will significantly contribute to nurturing the next generation of cyber security experts in Bangladesh.
